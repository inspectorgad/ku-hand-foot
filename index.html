üìñ Hand & Foot Rules

üéØ Objective

Score the most points over 4 rounds by creating melds (groups of cards) and canastas (7-card melds). The first player to play all cards from both their Hand and Foot ends the round.

üÉè The Cards

Hand & Foot uses 4 standard decks plus 8 jokers (216 cards total).

  • Natural Cards: 4s through Aces ‚Äî used to build melds
  • Wild Cards: 2s and Jokers ‚Äî can substitute for any natural card
  • 3s: Special cards (red 3s = bonus, black 3s = blockers)

üéÆ Setup

  • Each player receives 11 cards for their Hand
  • Each player receives 11 cards for their Foot (set aside, face down)
  • Remaining cards form the draw pile

üìã Turn Structure

Each turn has three phases:

  • 1. Draw: Take 2 cards from the draw pile, OR pick up the entire discard pile (if you can use the top card)
  • 2. Meld: Optionally play melds or add to existing melds
  • 3. Discard: Place one card on the discard pile to end your turn

üì¶ Melds

A meld is 3+ cards of the same rank.

  • Must have more natural cards than wild cards
  • Minimum 3 cards to start a meld
  • Can add cards to existing melds on future turns
  • Each rank can only have one meld per player

üèÜ Canastas

A canasta is a meld of 7 or more cards.

  • Clean Canasta (Red): No wild cards ‚Äî worth 500 points
  • Dirty Canasta (Black): Contains wild cards ‚Äî worth 300 points

ü¶∂ Picking Up Your Foot

When you play all cards from your Hand, you immediately pick up your Foot and continue playing. You must be "in your Foot" before you can go out.

üö™ Going Out

To end the round, you must:

  • Be playing from your Foot (Hand must be empty)
  • Have at least one meld
  • Play or discard your last card

üìä Scoring

  • Card Points: Sum of all cards in your melds
  • Clean Canasta: +500 bonus
  • Dirty Canasta: +300 bonus
  • Going Out: +100 bonus
  • Cards Left in Hand/Foot: Negative points (subtracted)

ü§ñ AI Difficulty

  • Easy: Makes basic plays, doesn't plan ahead
  • Medium: Builds toward canastas, smarter discards
  • Hard: Strategic play, tracks your discards, aggressive canasta building
